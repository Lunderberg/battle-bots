std::unique_ptr<RestrictedGameState> Actor::state
protected

The RestrictedGameState held by the current Actor.

Contains a view into the full GameState, containing only what the current Actor can see. At initialization, state contains a nullptr. After being added to a GameState, shows a valid RestrictedGameState.
